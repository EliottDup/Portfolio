Me

My name is Eliott Duparc, I am 17 years old and a student at the Metis Montessori Lyceum in Amsterdam. I was born in France, but later moved to the Netherlands. my main hobbies are gaming, programming, 3D modeling/art and freerunning. On this portfolio website I will show off most of the projects I've worked on or am working on now.

Projects

Here are some different projects I have worked on or am currently working on.

For the 2023 gmtk game jam, themed "Roles Reversed", me and a friend created MazeSwap, a 2-player game where the two players are stuck in a maze and both try to escape and stop the other player.
There are two roles in the game, Runner and Hunter. The runner tries to escape the maze by hitting a switch and finding the exit, and the hunter tries to get close enough to the runner to swap roles with them, and take on the runner's role themself.

currently, I am working together with a classmate to attempt to create an AI that can manage traffic lights. We are not far into this project and do not really know how this will turn out, but we will have to see.
